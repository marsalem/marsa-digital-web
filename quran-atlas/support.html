Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add a verse? Navigate into any topic or subtopic, then tap the + Verse button.
  • How do I back up my data? Go to Settings (⚙️) → Export Data to save a backup file.
  • How do I share a topic? Open any topic and tap the Share button to generate a shareable link.
  • Is the Quran text included? Yes — the full Quran with English translation is downloaded automatically on first launch.
  • Does it work in Arabic? Yes — the entire app is available in Arabic, including topics, search, and interface.
  • How do I switch languages? Tap the English / عربي toggle in the top bar at any time.
